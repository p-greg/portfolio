About Me

Introduction

My name is Colin Gregory. I was born in Billings, MT and moved to Portland in 2018. I received my undergraduate degree in computer science from Rocky Mountain College. Right now, I am pursuing my masters degree in software engineering at Portland State University.

Family

My family is most important to me. My parents, Susan and Paul, have always inspired me to be my best. They are both professors at universities in Billings. I have one sister who is four years older than me. She currently lives in Broomfield, CO and teaches 2nd grade.

Hobbies

My biggest hobby outside of programming would be soccer. Both my parents being British, I grew up around it and slowly became obsessed. My favorite team in Manchester United and my favorite player of all time is David Beckham.

When I am not watching soccer or programming, you can find me playing video games like FIFA, CS-GO, or PUBG. I play both console and PC.
